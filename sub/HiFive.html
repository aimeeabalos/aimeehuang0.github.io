Hi5
"is a playful experience that gives a space for students to interact and communicate
in order to encourage them to understand and embrace diversity."

"How to reduce the barriers and increase the interaction
among people from different cultural background?"





"Bring People Together"

Instead of dividing each other with Race/Nation, Hi5 celebrates the differences of the cultures. It targets to remove the invisible barriers such as stereotypes and prejudice.
