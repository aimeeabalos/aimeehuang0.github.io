VRoom x Frog

How might VR/AR allow us (designers) to collaboratively design spatial experiences?

VRoom is a VR app that helps designers collaboratively design spatial experience and provides a real-time preview for clients. It offers designers the ability to view their designs in Virtual Reality, make modifications, and share them with clients in real-time.

Group Members: Aimee Abalos, Elena Zhizhimontova, Brett Leibowitz, Benjamin Vick, Nicholas Quigley
