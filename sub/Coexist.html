"How to survive in Anthropocene"
COMING SOON
A game designed for Wildlife by Aimee Abalos

“How might we use technologies to build player’s system thinking on wildlife-conservation and human-wildlife conflict management?”

A animal-centered game that allows the user to play through the eye of Polar Bear to experience their struggle of living in their habitat. The project intends to evoke players empathy by shifting their role from human to other living species who are sharing the same resources on the same planet.

Watch the trailer here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fMU8ns6WNvM
