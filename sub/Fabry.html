Fabry

AR App for fashion lovers

Fabry Enhance the fashion show experience for the audience, by connecting physical feelings, such as
touching, with Augmented Reality technology, in order to let the fashion lovers engage more with the garment design process.
WHO
Fashion lovers
WHEN
Along with the runway show
WHERE
Space outside of the runway stage
HOW
Smart phones and tablets, AR, pattern recognition
